About the game


Cards for Humanity is a physical card game about the 17 UN goals set in 2015. During the game players take the role of a prospective UN president. Players will have to think of new solutions to the many modern day problems that are related to the UN goals. By coming up with the best solution a player can earn votes, the player that ends up with the most votes will win the round and is one step closer to becoming UN president.

Donations

OHCHR: UN Human rights office

Your donation can assist a victim of sexual violence to obtain justice, support the work of human rights monitors during elections, contribute to an investigation of a serious human rights violation or make a difference for human rights defenders.

OCHA: Central Emergency Response Fund

One of the most effective ways to provide time-critical assistance, including supplies, basic services and protection to millions of people in need.

UNRWA: Protection for Palestine refugees

Donate to support UNRWAs mission to help Palestine refugees achieve their full potential, pending a just and lasting solution to their plight.

World Food Programme

Donate now and the World Food Programme will keep you updated on how your generosity is making a difference around the world.

UNICEF: United Nations Children's Fund

With your donation, you are working together with UNICEF for the survival, protection and development of children in more than 190 countries around the world.

UNHCR: Un Refugee Agency

You can help rush critical supplies like medicine, clean water, food and tents to desperate children and families.

UN Women

UN Women works for the elimination of discrimination against women and girls, the empowerment of women, human rights, humanitarian action, and peace and security.

UNESCO: World heritage centre

Contributions to UNESCOs World Heritage Centre made by individuals like you are used for projects implemented by experts to protect World Heritage sites around the world.

UN OPS: united nations office for project services

UNOPS helps the UN, governments and other partners to manage projects, and deliver sustainable infrastructure and procurement in an efficient way.

UNFPA: united nations population fund

Your donation can help UNFPA deliver a world where every pregnancy is wanted, every childbirth is safe and every young person's potential is fulfilled.

UNAIDS

Ensure that mothers can protect themselves and their children from HIV; Help young people take leadership in the AIDS response so that services meet their needs; Support people living with HIV to access care and support, etc.

IOM: International organization for migration

Your donation to IOM will help vulnerable migrants, refugees, internally displaced persons, survivors of human trafficking, exploitation, and gender-based violence and those fleeing poverty, conflict and natural disasters.
